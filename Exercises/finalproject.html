Young Adult Nonfiction Mental Health Titles

Purpose of the Core Collection:

The goal of the core collection is to fill a collection gap, in this case, to build a specific young adult nonfiction collection of mental health resources.

Selection Criteria:
Skokie Public Library’s (SPL) Resource Selection Policy weighed heavily into the criteria used in making these core collection selections of mental health titles for teens. Many of these elements come from SPL’s Mission Statement and Core Service Values, which promote personal growth, discovery, cultivating learning and literacy, engaging the community, the exchange of ideas through a broad spectrum of materials, technologies, and experiences.
